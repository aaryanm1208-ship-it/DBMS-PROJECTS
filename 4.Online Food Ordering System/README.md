# Online Food Ordering System

**Description:**  
Customers can order food online and restaurants can manage orders.

**Key Features:**  
- Customer registration  
- Restaurant & menu management  
- Place and track orders  
- Generate billing reports

**Tables:**  
- Customers (CustomerID, Name, Email, Phone)  
- Restaurants (RestaurantID, Name, Location)  
- Menu (MenuID, RestaurantID, DishName, Price)  
- Orders (OrderID, CustomerID, RestaurantID, OrderDate, TotalAmount)
